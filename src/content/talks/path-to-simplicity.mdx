As software engineers, we’re often surrounded by chaos, and the entropy levels between product, sales, design, and engineering teams are typically high. While we may not have ownership of the entire pipeline, we do possess ownership of the code. With this ownership, we have the power to simplify our environment. Let’s discuss how fine-grained reactivity can guide us on the path to simplicity.
